07/21/2023

Book review: Gory Details by Erika Engelhaupt

I enjoyed this book a lot. As I've gotten older and more busy I appreciate books with more episodic concepts that are easy to put down and pick back up, and the focus on a new story each time was engaging. I enjoyed the tieback of every story at the epilogue where the purpose of studying and cataloguing these discoveries was acknowledged as necessary for people to learn and have a healthier attitude towards the world around us and ourselves. Overall, highly recommend if you aren't squeamish and are interested in more bizarre scientific discoveries being made, or if you are squeamish and want to build your tolerance. There are no images and descriptions are kept fairly brief, but there are more detailed drawings to section off the book.
